Tomalistis Falls

Tomalistis Falls is a breathtaking natural gem located in Barangay Asug, Caibiran, Biliran. Known for its pristine waters and serene surroundings, the falls is a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Nestled amidst lush greenery, this stunning cascade offers visitors a tranquil retreat and a glimpse of Biliran's untouched beauty. Tomalistis Falls is not just a tourist attraction—it is a symbol of the community's harmonious relationship with nature. The crystal-clear waters flow generously, representing the lifeblood of Barangay Asug, supporting its agricultural and daily needs. Managed and preserved by the local community, Tomalistis Falls is a testament to the people’s dedication to protecting their natural resources. Visitors are welcomed warmly and encouraged to experience the beauty and peace that this hidden paradise offers, all while respecting its fragile ecosystem. Whether you're looking to enjoy a refreshing dip, a quiet moment in nature, or an adventure to remember, Tomalistis Falls is your destination for natural wonders and heartfelt connections.
